# MASSBD Job Training - Mobile App

A Progressive Web App (PWA) for **MASSBD Market Place** that lets you access job training, online income opportunities, and educational programs directly from your mobile phone — installable to your home screen!

---

## 📱 What This App Does

- **Quick Access** to all MASSBD programs from one mobile-friendly home screen
- **One-tap navigation** to Job Training, Online Income, Digital Marketing, Freelancing, BCS Prep, and more
- **Installable** — add it to your phone's home screen like a native app
- **Works offline** — cached for faster loading even with slow internet
- **Native app feel** — splash screen, bottom navigation, swipe gestures

---

## 🚀 How to Install

### Android (Chrome)
1. Open the app in **Google Chrome**
2. Tap the **menu (⋮)** → **"Add to Home Screen"** or **"Install App"**
3. Tap **"Install"** — the app icon will appear on your home screen!

### iPhone / iPad (Safari)
1. Open the app in **Safari**
2. Tap the **Share button** (⬆️) at the bottom
3. Scroll down and tap **"Add to Home Screen"**
4. Tap **"Add"** — done!

### Samsung Internet
1. Tap the **menu (⋮)** → **"Add page to"** → **"Home screen"**
